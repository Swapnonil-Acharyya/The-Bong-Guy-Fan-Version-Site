Who is The Bong Guy?

Kiran Dutta



Kiran Dutta known as The Bong Guy is the first Bengali content creator.
He was born on 15 July 1995 in Dhubulia, West Bengal. He has two channels in Youtube by the name of The Bong Guy which is the main channel where he post comedy and music videos while the second channel is named by Your Bong Guy.
